# Chit Scheme Feature

The Chit Scheme feature allows users to track periodic contribution schemes (like Chits, Committees, or ROSCA) directly within their Financial Goals.

## How it Works

When creating a new financial goal, you can toggle the **Chit Scheme** option. This unlocks specific fields for:

- **Monthly Amount**: The amount you contribute each month.
- **Duration (Months)**: The total number of months the scheme will run.

### Automatic Calculations

For Chit schemes, the app automatically calculates:

1.  **Target Amount**: Derived from `Monthly Amount * Duration`.
2.  **Deadline**: Automatically set based on the duration from the start date.

## Tracking Progress

In the **Goals List**, Chit schemes are marked with a distinct **CHIT** badge. The monthly contribution amount is displayed on the goal card, making it easy to see your monthly commitments at a glance.

## Editing a Chit Goal

You can edit a Chit goal by tapping on it in the list. You can update the monthly amount or duration, and the target amount will adjust automatically.
